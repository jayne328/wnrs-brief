Video Guidelines
What great WNRS content looks like
✓  Do
✓ Show product in the first 5 seconds
✓ Speak naturally — never scripted
✓ Tie into a real moment (dinner, road trip, date night)
✓ Keep gameplay visible and easy to follow
✓ Add on-screen captions for clarity
✓ Reply to comments — build Part 2 series
✓ Let emotional reactions land — don't rush through
✗  Don't
✗ Open with "Ad" or sales language
✗ Only show packaging — show the cards in action
✗ Rely only on discount or flash-sale messaging
✗ Read cards in a monotone voice
✗ Rush through the question before it lands
✗ Use AI-generated content
